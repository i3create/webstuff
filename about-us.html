We Are

i3create is an information technology (IT) consulting company that serves in the main, small and medium-sized enterprises in Africa. We provide sustainable technology advice to our clients by leveraging our deep and industry-wide experience of currently trending technologies.



Quickly Transform Your Business With Us

When you choose i3create to help you with your IT challenges, you get in return - structured technology advice that will accelerate your firm's transformation. We utilise trending technologies to help you gain an advantage over your competitors. At i3create we effeciently reinvent your operations, enabling you to take advantage of current and future opportnunities.

A detailed IT strategy is important to the success of any business. The systems and process you have should work hard for you, making your business run more smoothly. We will help to device your strategy and make your business more effective and profitable.
